# ğŸ›‚ Passports

Weâ€™ve been looking for a creative way for people to be â€œcard-carrying membersâ€ of Purdue Hackers and have a physical keepsake from their time in college. 3 months ago, we began prototyping Purdue Hackers passports, and last month we began mass-producing them.

[View a 1-minute tour of a passport](https://youtu.be/AngfUtdNcV8?si=4EdVDwEkVV3D42TB)

Every passport has an NFC sticker embedded in its cover. Hackers [design their own data page](https://passports.purduehackers.com), then assemble their own passports at hour-long passport-making ceremonies, which run every two weeks at Hack Night. We then write to their NFC sticker with [an iOS app](https://github.com/purduehackers/passport-authority) we made. Finally, hackers stamp their passports with a new stamp every week. Over 40 hackers have made a passport so far.
